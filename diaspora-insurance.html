Traveler Types

Outbound and inbound cover routes.

Choose cover according to who is traveling, why they are traveling, and whether they are moving alone, with a school, with a company, or as a group.

Outbound Travelers

  • Regular individual travelers.
  • Corporate clients and company staff traveling for business or leisure.
  • School trips abroad.
  • Groups attending seminars or workshops.

Inbound Travelers

  • Student cover for students traveling abroad for studies or organized trips.
  • Individual cover for business or pleasure trips, short-term or long-term.
  • Group cover for 10 or more people traveling on an identical itinerary.
  • Corporate cover for staff, supported by deposit premium at inception.
