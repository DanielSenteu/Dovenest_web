Education Insurance Overview

A savings path for their future.

The purpose of an education insurance policy is to serve as a savings vehicle that may be converted into a lump sum payment to cover your child's university or college tuition. Whether you are present or not, this form of insurance can be utilized to save money for your children's education.

Despite the large variety of educational plans available from insurance companies, few people are fully aware of what an educational insurance policy entails. DoveNest helps make the options clearer.

1

Save toward university or college tuition

2

Include an investment component where suitable

3

Protect the child against unexpected accident or hospitalization

4

Start with a low-cost premium and grow over time

Our Focus

In Kenya, it is usually a good idea to choose an insurance plan that includes an investing component, because it allows the child to grow savings while adding a protection layer.
