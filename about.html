Who We Are

Independent Advisory.
Accountable at Every Stage.

DoveNest Insurance Brokers is an independent advisory firm established to serve individuals, institutions, and diaspora clients across Kenya. We do not simply arrange policies. We diagnose risk, structure cover, negotiate terms, and actively support claims outcomes.

Our independence allows us to select underwriting partners based on financial strength, claims responsiveness, and sector fit. Our clients are not matched to products. Products are structured around their exposures.

How Our Team Works Around You

  • Each client is assigned a named advisor accountable for the structure and performance of their cover
  • Cover is built to reflect actual risk exposure, not minimum compliance requirements
  • Claims support is active, not passive. We track outcomes and intervene where needed
  • Reviews are structured, periodic, and aligned to changes in your business or personal circumstances
