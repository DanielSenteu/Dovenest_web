Who We Are

Your Broker Is Only as Good as Your Last Claim.

DoveNest Insurance Brokers is an independent advisory firm providing structured insurance solutions to individuals, businesses, and institutions in Kenya and across the diaspora.

We do not simply arrange policies. We diagnose risk, structure cover, negotiate terms, and actively support claims outcomes. Our approach ensures that protection is not only in place, but fit for purpose when tested.

Our Process

How We Work

Every solution we structure follows a disciplined advisory framework, from initial risk diagnosis to active claims stewardship.

01

Risk Mapping

We identify actual exposures across operations, assets, people and third-party interactions, not assumed ones.

02

Cover Structuring

We align insurance design to operational realities. Cover is built around your actual risk profile, not off-the-shelf assumptions.

03

Underwriter Negotiation

We negotiate terms, extensions and service-level commitments upfront, ensuring your cover performs before you need it.

04

Claims Stewardship

We remain actively involved to ensure claims are handled promptly, fairly and in line with agreed expectations.
