Eligibility Conditions

Who the product covers.

The product covers a family consisting of a maximum of one main member, one spouse, four children, two parents, and two parents-in-law.

1

Minimum Entry Age

Main member: 18 years. Spouse: 18 years. Children: 14 days. Parents / parents-in-law: 18 years.

2

Maximum Entry Age

Main member: 70 years. Spouse: 70 years. Children: 24 years. Parents / parents-in-law: 75 years.

3

Cover Cease Age

Main member: none. Spouse: none. Children: 24 years if the child is a student at a registered institution. Parents / parents-in-law: none.

4

Waiting Period

Three months waiting period applies.
